Q & A (FAQ)

Could you help me to integrate QuickIO.NET into my project(s)?

QuickIO.NET offers near the same signature of classes and methodes like the .NET System.IO namespace. It's very easy to integrate QuickIO.NET. Please understand that I cannot offer assisted support. Contact a forum of your choice.

  • For the German-speaking world I recommend myCsharp.de.
  • For all English speaking I recommend StackOverflow (use tag QuickIONET).

Is this project for sale?

No.

Are there any code examples?

Yes. QuickIO.NET is well documentated. Please check the code documentation of the specified class or method. Most of them are attended by samples. Or check http://quickio.net/Examples

Why QuickIO.NET was created

I was asked by some, why I've developed such a "brilliant" library.

First of all, thank you to everyone who trust QuickIO.NET. I have about five years experimented with file operations in my free time and programmed some methods into a small library. After a family bereavement in early 2014 (R.I.P. Max), I need to distract me and developed from the bottom up QuickIO.NET.

That's the reason why QuickIO.NET was created.

Will Mono be supported?

I am a software developer with focused on high-performance web applications in ASP.NET, cloud and Microsoft Azure. I have no contact with Linux environments. Therefore, I have lost track of Mono support. If someone agreed to integrate Mono support, so please get in touch with me.
